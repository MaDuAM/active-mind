# ActiveMind

## A Full-Stack Project with Depth

ActiveMind is more than just another todo or knowledge management app. It is the result of an intensive collaboration between human systems thinking and AI-assisted development — and a personal tool for managing the growing flood of information, strategies, and insights generated by daily AI use.

---

## Why This Project?

I built ActiveMind for several interconnected reasons:

**1. A Showcase of My Skills**  
As a graduate in Applied Computer Science (B.Sc.), I want to demonstrate that I can not only write code but also design and implement complex systems — from database architecture and backend logic to responsive user interfaces.

**2. A Full-Stack Implementation**  
The app covers the entire modern stack: TypeScript, React, Tailwind, Express, Prisma, PostgreSQL. It is not a toy project but a well-architected, scalable, production-ready application.

**3. An Unconventional Perspective**  
The structure — dividing entries into Knowledge, Passive, and Active areas — deliberately breaks with traditional task lists. It reflects a mindset that views actions not as isolated todos but as part of a larger system. This unusual angle tends to attract attention and spark conversation.

**4. A Tool for My Own Use**  
For over a year, I have been working with AI daily. The volume of extracted strategies, insights, and actionable ideas grows faster than I can structure it. ActiveMind is my attempt to tame this information overload — to capture knowledge, track strategies, and ensure nothing important gets lost.

---

## How I Built It — and What I Learned

I did not build this project alone. I worked closely with AI (specifically ChatGPT) — not as a substitute for my knowledge, but as a catalyst. This collaboration taught me how to use AI efficiently, where its limits are, and how to achieve better results faster through structured interaction. Without this partnership, the project would have taken months — and might have failed due to its complexity.

This is not a weakness but a genuine competency: mastering AI-assisted development is just as relevant today as writing code itself.

From initial concept to a production-ready prototype: approximately **4 weeks of intensive work** (including ideation, technology selection, and implementation). The first few days were spent entirely on exploring ideas, understanding the app market, and refining the concept. Once the technical specification was set, development progressed quickly.

## Key Features

- **Three Entry Types:** Knowledge (static), Passive (trackable actions), Active (step-by-step processes)
- **Status Management:** WAITING, ACTIVE, PAUSED — with tracking history
- **Soft Delete & Trash:** Restore or permanently remove entries
- **Full-Text Search:** With suggestions and result layers
- **Tracking Log:** Every status change, step update, and edit is recorded
- **Responsive Design:** Fully optimized for desktop, tablet (coming soon), and mobile
- **Dark Mode:** System-aware theme toggle
- **Pagination & Infinite Scroll:** For large entry sets
